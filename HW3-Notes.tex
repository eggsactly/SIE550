$$det(A-I\lambda)=0$$

$$P=\begin{bmatrix} p_{11} & p_{12} \\

$$\ubar{x}=\begin{bmatrix} x_{1} \\

$$V(\ubar{x})=\ubar{x}^TP\ubar{x}$$
